How It Works

Live Routing

Calculates the most efficient driving route between any two points using the Open Source Routing Machine (OSRM).

Hazard Detection

Scans global disaster databases (GDACS) for major events like earthquakes and cyclones near your path.

AI-Powered Analysis

Uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to read real-time news feeds, identify local hazards, and determine if they are relevant to your journey.
